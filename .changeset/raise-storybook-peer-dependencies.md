Raised peer dependency ranges for `storybook` and `@storybook/addon-docs` from `^10.0.0` to `^10.3.5`. Installations on Storybook 10.3.4 or earlier no longer satisfy peers and must upgrade Storybook (and matching addons) to at least 10.3.5.
